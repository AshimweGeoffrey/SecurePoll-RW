Small label for status, category, or counts. Tone carries meaning — reuse the verification palette (`green` approved, `amber` review, `red` rejected).

```jsx
<Badge tone="green" dot>Registered</Badge>
<Badge tone="amber" variant="solid">Pending sync</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" variant="outline">Constituency 14</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral` · `green` · `blue` · `amber` · `red`. Variants: `soft` (default) · `solid` · `outline`. `dot` adds a leading status dot; `size` is `sm` or `md`.
